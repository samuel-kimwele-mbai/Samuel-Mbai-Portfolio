Students Hub

Students Hub is a secure, Android-based application developed as my final-year project to assist university students in collaborating on academic projects, sharing resources, and managing study groups efficiently. The app addresses common challenges students face in organizing and managing their academic work, offering a structured platform for collaboration and communication.


The application features secure user authentication through Firebase, ensuring only authorized users can access the platform. Students can create and join project workspaces, assign tasks, and track progress in real time. It also includes a resource-sharing feature, allowing users to upload and access academic materials, facilitating easy exchange of information within study groups.

promote smooth communication, Students Hub integrates a real-time messaging system, enabling users to discuss ideas and share updates within their project teams. The app also incorporates task management tools, helping students stay organized and meet deadlines effectively.


Built using Android Studio, Firebase, and MySQL, the app ensures a seamless, secure user experience while demonstrating my skills in mobile app development and problem-solving.
